## Stock Status System

The latest version introduces a powerful **Stock Status Management System** that allows you to:

- Manage product availability with predefined statuses
- Customize stock statuses to fit your business needs
- Control UI visibility based on stock status
- Extend functionality with custom hooks and filters

### Available Stock Statuses

- **In Stock** - Products available for immediate purchase
- **Out of Stock** - Products currently unavailable
- **On Order** - Products available for pre-order
- **Expected** - Products expected to arrive soon
